Safia Abdalla is an open source developer and writer with a passion for community building and knowledge sharing.

Who Is Safia

Safia is building Zarf, an online platform for independent writers producing subscription content. Safia is also producing The Zarf Audio Log, a podcast that shares the story of building the product and the business around it. When she's not doing that, she can be found drinking black tea, reading on her Kindle, or running outside.

Safia maintains open source projects like legit, goops, commentator, checklist, brief, giddy, fony, and many more. Safia likes building minimal but productive and powerful tools.

Where Safia Speaks

Storytelling and speaking are Safia's favorite ways to share knowledge. She has presented keynotes, workshops, technical talks, and non-technical talks at a variety of conferences. She's also appeared on a couple of podcasts too. Check them out at this page!
